Our Story

Since our establishment in 1966, we've been dedicated to crafting exceptional traditional home furniture. Under the visionary leadership of Takis Charalambous, our company swiftly rose to prominence in Cyprus for its top-notch products.

In 1970, we expanded by establishing our first full-scale production plant at Yerolakkos Industrial Area. Takis Charalambous saw the need for innovative business furniture that truly meets the demands of modern workplaces. Despite challenges, we've emerged stronger. Today, from our private factory with advanced CNC machinery and modern warehouse facilities, we thrive, thanks to our loyal customers' trust.
